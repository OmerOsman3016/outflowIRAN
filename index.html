About data & methodology

Source

DTM Sudan Cross-Border Monitoring Report, reporting period January 2024 to December 2025.

Coverage

This dashboard uses six monitored cross-border points: Argeen, Ashkeet, Um Dukhun, Adekonk-Ag Geneina, At Tina, and Gallabat/Metema.

Method

Flow Monitoring Count captures total crossings into Sudan, place of origin, intended destination, and mode of travel. Flow Monitoring Survey provides individual-level information.

Important caveat

This map uses indicative destination anchors for visualization. It should not be interpreted as a full monthly destination matrix.

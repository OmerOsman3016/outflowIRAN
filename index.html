About data & methodology

Source

DTM Sudan Cross-Border Monitoring Report (2), publication date 15 February 2026, reporting period January 2024 to December 2025.

Coverage

This dashboard is based on six monitored cross-border points: Argeen, Ashkeet, Um Dukhun, Adekonk-Ag Geneina, At Tina, and Gallabat/Metema.

How the data were collected

Flow Monitoring Count records the number of individuals crossing into Sudan, their place of origin, intended destination, and mode of travel. Flow Monitoring Survey collects individual-level information, and final findings were weighted using Flow Monitoring Count volumes.

Important note

Data validity dates vary by cross-border point. Figures are preliminary and may be revised. Destination points shown on this map are visualization anchors, not a full monthly destination matrix.
